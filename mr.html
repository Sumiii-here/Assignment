Mr.Bean: The Animated Series

Episode-1

After Mr. Bean accidentally drops Teddy into a bucket full of extremely smelly sardines, Teddy becomes very stinky, causing Bean to bring him to the laundry shop.

That night, Bean sees a goldfish in the pond outside about to be eaten by Scrapper, but manages to rescue the goldfish and takes care of him.

The next day, Bean takes him to the beach until he gets dragged when a swordfish eats the goldfish.

Upon landing at a fishing boat, Bean sees his goldfish alive but becomes stinky and is forced to go to the laundry shop to wash himself like Teddy.
